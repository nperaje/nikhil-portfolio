Slack + Egnyte → Transform → Looker Studio

A Google Apps Script ETL automation that supports a Data Operations team in processing SaaS contract purchase and invoice files uploaded by customers. It ingests file upload activity from Egnyte and team communications from Slack, cleans and transforms the raw data, and loads it into Google Sheets connected to a Looker Studio dashboard — giving the team real-time visibility to prioritise and act on newly uploaded contract files.
